## 3. 各风格详细说明

### 01. Minimalism & Swiss Style（極簡主義 & 瑞士風格）

- **类型**：一般
- **关键词**：Clean, simple, spacious, functional, white space, high contrast, geometric, sans-serif, grid-based, essential
- **视觉主色**：Monochromatic, Black #000000, White #FFFFFF
- **辅助色/层次**：Neutral (Beige #F5F1E8, Grey #808080, Taupe #B38B6D), Primary accent
- **动效与质感**：Subtle hover (200-250ms), smooth transitions, sharp shadows if any, clear type hierarchy, fast loading
- **性能**：⚡ Excellent
- **无障碍**：✓ WCAG AAA
- **暗色模式支持**：✓ Full
- **实现复杂度**：Low
- **适用场景**：Enterprise apps (企業應用), dashboards (儀表板), documentation sites (網站), SaaS platforms (SaaS), professional tools (工具)
- **不建议场景**：Creative portfolios (創意), entertainment (娛樂), playful brands (品牌), artistic experiments
- **框架兼容建议**：Tailwind 10/10, Bootstrap 9/10, MUI 9/10
- **风格年代/来源**：1950s Swiss
- **本地案例**：`refstyles/01-minimalism.html`
- **在线预览**：[打开案例](https://yuanfu8899.github.io/uiuxskillProMax/refstyles/01-minimalism.html)

- **性能**：❌ Poor

- **暗色模式支持**：◐ Partial
- **实现复杂度**：High
- **适用场景**：Legacy apps (應用程式), gaming (遊戲), immersive storytelling, premium products (產品), luxury, realistic simulations, education
- **不建议场景**：Modern enterprise, critical accessibility (無障礙性), low-performance (效能), web (use Flat/Modern) (網頁)
- **框架兼容建议**：CSS-in-JS 7/10, Custom 8/10
